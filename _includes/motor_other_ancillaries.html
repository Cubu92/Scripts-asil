Motor Legal Protection  

Just to make you aware, this policy doesn’t include Motor Legal Protection cover.

Cover Summary

 

 

If you or any of the named drivers were involved in an accident in this vehicle that wasn’t your fault, you wouldn’t be covered for help to claim compensation for injuries, travel expenses, loss of earnings or the policy excess from the person who caused the accident. 

As long as we think your claim is likely to succeed, Motor Legal Protection will cover legal costs up to £100,000 to pursue your claim.

Without this cover, we can still arrange legal assistance for you, or you can find your own solicitor to help with the claim, or you can pursue a claim on your own.

However, there are normally costs associated with doing this, and in many cases legal firms are also entitled to make a deduction from any settlement. With Motor Legal Protection you keep 100% of any settlement.
