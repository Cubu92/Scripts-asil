Associating /Consolidating ACTIVE MOTOR RISKS (Non-Telematics) to join a new policy.  

 

  • MUST SPEAK TO PH OR PERSON THAT SET UP THE ORIGINAL POLICY TO ASSOCIATE.

 

“You accepted/renewed your existing motor policy on the (DATE), before I copy the information over, are you happy the details we hold on file are still accurate and nothing has changed”?

No? - Check ALL details                                                                     Yes? - See below:

 

I’ll give you some information about the cover we provide, the full list of benefits are available on our website is that ok? (Must gain consent)            

(If NO – Give prices but explain “We won’t be able to accept any cover over the phone unless you agree to receive limited information.  If you prefer to see the full terms & conditions beforehand, these are available on our website or you can have information sent by post and I’ll call you back?”)

 

Once associated/consolidated:    ***CHECK COVER START DATE OF ASSOCIATED POLICY***

  • Confirm Make, Model & Reg with phonetics.

 

  • Confirm names of ALL drivers.

 

  • Note any existing Claims/Convictions to cross reference on new quote.

 

  • “Have you or any named driver had any motoring offences including fixed penalties, convictions, or disqualifications during the year, or have any pending prosecutions?” 

 

  • “Have you or any named driver had any accidents, incidents, thefts or losses, or made any claims, fault or non-fault, during the year?”

 

  • Existing policy Classic car? (May need Level 9 review)

 “This policy will continue as an agreed value policy, please notify us if the agreed value changes.

 

  • Ancillaries on existing policy? – “You currently have the following additional benefits on your policy (Full KCs of each), would you like to include these again?”  (Must gain consent)
