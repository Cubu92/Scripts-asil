  • Ensure the ‘effective’ date is today’s date

 

Has an online quote been completed for the FDC?

Was the online quote a Telematics quote?

Yes – Must set up as a new policy unless customer led. To add as an FDC the new Telematics risk MUST be covered for more than 150 days and the existing policy must be in its first term.

No – Continue below
