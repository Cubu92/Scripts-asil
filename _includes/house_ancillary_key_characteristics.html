In an emergency that results in total failure, optional Home Emergency cover provides up to £500 for call out charges, parts and labour, to make temporary repair to your hot water, central heating or internal plumbing, as well as cover for roofing damage and pest infestation. This also includes Boiler emergency cover and offers unlimited callouts.

Claims can be made 7 days after this cover starts and MUST be reported to us within 48 hours discovery.    Incidents prior to the start date are not covered.

 

Rental property - Home Emergency may duplicate cover in your rental agreement, and you should get the landlord or owner’s permission before carrying out any work.
