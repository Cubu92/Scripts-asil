Delivery guidelines (ALL) 

 

  • Advise how many levels of cover are available.
  • Ask all qualifying questions before delivering any Tier or Ancillary KCs
  • Must advise lowest/mid-range/highest tier each time a tier is delivered.
  • Must gain limited disclosure consent BEFORE tier KCs are delivered. (Once only, so may have been delivered as part of motor negotiation)
  • (IF ADDING A 2nd HOME MAKE SURE DUPLICATE FLP PROCESS IS FOLLOWED)

 

Accidental damage.   

  • Ensure caller is aware of AD cover provided by each level of cover for either/both contents/buildings and if downgrading ensure the PH is given an opportunity to add increased AD cover if applicable. 

Excess

Compulsory and Voluntary excesses must be provided.

  • Additional excesses may apply depending on claim cause and could exceed the amount you are claiming for. 
  • ·         Are you happy to read these in your Home Policy Schedule?  
  • ·         If delivering additional compulsory excesses must advise these are in addition to voluntary excess.
  • Must gain consent
